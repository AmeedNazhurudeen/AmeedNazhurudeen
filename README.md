## About

I build autonomous robotic systems that operate where GPS and clean data don't reach — underwater, in the field, or in unstructured industrial spaces. My background spans underwater ROV/AUV development, multi-sensor fusion (LiDAR, IMU, depth), SLAM, and AI-based perception, with hands-on time in both research labs and industrial automation.

Currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Mobile Robotics at the University of Bonn, building on a B.Eng. in Mechatronics and two years of applied robotics work in India — including leading R&D on an autonomous underwater ROV for coral reef monitoring.
